About Us

London's Most Trusted Plumbing & Building Service

With over 25 years of experience in plumbing and building refurbishments, we've earned the trust of thousands of London homeowners and businesses.

SW Builds & Plumbing has been serving London and the surrounding areas for over two decades. We specialise in all aspects of plumbing, bathroom & kitchen refurbishments, loft conversions, and home extensions. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional results on every project, whether it's a simple repair or a complex renovation.

We pride ourselves on our reliability, transparency, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Every member of our team is fully trained, certified, and insured. We use only the best materials and equipment to ensure your project is completed to the highest standards.

From emergency plumbing calls at 3am to full house extensions, we handle it all with the same level of professionalism and attention to detail.
