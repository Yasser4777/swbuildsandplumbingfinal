About Us

London's Trusted Builders & Plumbers Since 1999

Founded in 1999, SW Builds & Plumbing specialises in plumbing services, bathroom and kitchen refurbishments, loft conversions, and home extensions. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver exceptional results for every project.

Our team of qualified plumbers, builders, and specialists brings decades of combined experience to transform your home. Whether you need emergency plumbing repairs, a complete bathroom makeover, a modern kitchen refurbishment, a loft conversion to add space, or a home extension — we're committed to quality, reliability, and your complete satisfaction.
